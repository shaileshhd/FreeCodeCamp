General

We all have seen a character interacts with hologram virtual objects, Head Mount Display (HMD) in sci-fi movies. Augmented Reality (AR) technology is improving over the past couple of years which brings sci-fi tech to sci-based reality.

AR is one of the biggest technology trends right now, and it is only going to get bigger as AR-ready smartphones and other devices become more accessible around the world which lets us see the real-life environment right in front of us with a digital augmentation overlaid on it. In other words, AR is a technology that augments the user's reality by blending the real and digital world in a subtle which will provide the user with a unique experience.

Technology has advanced to the point where realism in virtual reality is very achievable. One must capture the imagination to create truly compelling experiences. On the spectrum between virtual reality, which creates immersible, computer-generated environments, and the real world, augmented reality is closer to the real world. It is important to determine the current situation of AR, used in various sectors and divided into various applications, software, hardware, and to predict the future developments that can be experienced.

Overview

This revolutionized AR tech is beneficial for construction, architects, designers, and project management. AR in construction makes it possible to combine virtual architectural designs with the physical reality of a job site. AR increases accuracy and efficiency by reducing errors related to managing time, money, and resources.

SI NO. Virtual Reality Augmented Reality
1. Completely immersive virtual environment. The system augments the real-world scene.
2. In VR, visual senses are under the control of the system. In AR User always have a sense of presence in the real world.
3. VR is 75% virtual and 25% real. AR is 25% virtual and 75% real.
4. This technology fully immerses the user into the action. This technology partially immerses the user into the action.
5. Some VR headset device is needed. No AR headset is needed.
6. By VR technology, the user is isolated from the real world and immerses himself in a completely fictional world. With AR, end-users are still in touch with the real world while interacting with virtual objects nearer to them.
7. It is used to enhance fictional reality for the gaming world. It is used to enhance both real and virtual worlds.

Need for AR

Physical-world is three-dimensional whereas most of the data is trapped on twodimensional pages and screens. This gulf between the real and digital worlds prevents us from fully exploiting the volumes of information now available to us. Augmented reality, a set of technologies that superimposes digital data and images on physical objects and environments is closing this gap.
